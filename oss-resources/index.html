Tools¶

OSSPREY — Is your open source project on a sustainable path? OSSPREY uses AI to forecast the health of open source projects and gives you concrete, evidence-based recommendations for what to do about it. Point it at any GitHub repository and get month-by-month sustainability forecasts, actionable suggestions ranked by impact, and interactive visualizations of your project’s social and technical networks. Built by the DECAL Lab at UC Davis.
